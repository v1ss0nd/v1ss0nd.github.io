Commands

⠀

⠀

    ℹ️
  • #help - Link to this page.
  • #ping - Pings the bot.
  • #info [command] (e.g #info wiki) - Information about command.
  • #news [country] (e.g. #news ru) - Latest news from chosen country.
  • #weather [place] (e.g. #weather Krasnoyarsk) - Current weather of chosen place.
  • #time [region] [city] (e.g. #time Asia Omsk) - Current time in chosen place. (If command doesn't work, Check the list of known cities — paste.ivr.fi/oxupuxomug.kt)
  • #wiki [language] [item] (e.g. #wiki ru Гитлер) - Search something on Wikipedia.
  • #calc [number] [sign] [number] (e.g. #calc 2 * 744, #calc 1548 - 60) - Calculator
  • #spam [message] , [count] (e.g. #spam AlienFeel AlienPls , 5) - Spams message decided number of times. (Responds only to moderator messages.)
  • #randomnumber [min] [max] (e.g. #randomnumber 1 300) - Choose random number.
  • #randomword - Shows random word.
  • #randomemote - Shows one of 7000 emotes.
  • #randomsentence - Shows random sentence.
  • #thispepedoesnotexist - Shows a non-existent, ai generated pepe.
  • #thiscatdoesnotexist - Shows a non-existent, ai generated cat.
  • #thiswaifudoesnotexist - Shows a non-existent, ai generated waifu.
  • #uselessfact - Random useless fact.
  • #currency_rub - Shows current currency of RUB to USD.
  • #currency_uah - Shows current currency of UAH to USD.
  • #currency_cny - Shows current currency of CNY to USD.
  • #UkraineArmyLosses - Showing currently losses of Ukraine Army, with sources, updating once a month.
  • #RussianArmyLosses - Showing currently losses of Russian Army, with sources, updating once a month.
